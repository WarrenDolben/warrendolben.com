Bio

 I grew up on the Seacoast in Portsmouth, NH. I went to Alfred University in south western New York. I majored in English, and minored in Communications. I was also a four year member of the Saxons' swim team, and was part of a conference championship team in 2009, and won an individual championship in 2012. I coached collegiate swimming as an Assistant Coach for several years; first at King's College in Wilkes-Barre, PA, then at Washington College in Chestertown, MD. I am enjoying learning the intricacies of new technologies, and feel that my attention to detail, and desire to hone my craft, will well serve clients' needs.
 I've been on this learning journey for nearly seven years now. I first started building this site in 2017, and managed to finally get the site live a few years later (1.26.21), after spending some time on Wordpress. I made my first github commit July 12th 2018. It's a nice reminder of how far I've come, and also how long I've been at it!
 Feel free to follow my blog, where I'll be posting regularly about what I'm up to -- the most recent rendition of this can be found @ Web Dev Blog. Feel free to get into contact with me if you have an idea for a project. Thanks for stopping by.
