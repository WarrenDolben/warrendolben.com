I grew up on the Seacoast in Portsmouth, NH. I went to a small private university in South Western New York called Alfred University. I majored in English, and minored in Communications. I was also a four year member of the Saxons' swim team. I coached collegiate swimming as an Assistant Coach for several years; first at King's College in Wilkes-Barre, PA, then at Washington College in Chestertown, MD. I first started studying Web Development in November of this past year after brief stints as a blogger, personal editor, and salesman. I am enjoying learning the intricacies of new technologies, and feel that my attention to detail, and desire to hone my craft, will well serve clients' needs.
